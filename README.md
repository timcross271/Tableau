## Tableau Dashboards

#### - Satisfaction Score for NHS Users
Analysis of mock NHS patient data. Looking at the correlation of various factors with satisfaction score, and insights that can be gained to improve the satusfaction score in the future.

#### - Gold Prices: 1916-2025
Analysis of gold prices and percentage prices change per year. Looking at patterns of surges and crashes, and what this can tell about the current market situation in early 2026.
